### Brand feel

Earthy, warm, artisanal. The burgundy/brown/orange palette signals natural ingredients, handcraft, and groundedness — not sterile clinical beauty. Pair with:

- **Typography:** A warm serif for headings (e.g. *Playfair Display*, *Cormorant*, *DM Serif*) + a clean sans-serif for body (e.g. *Inter*, *Work Sans*).
- **Photography:** Natural light, warm tones, hands at work, textured surfaces (wood, fabric, Ankara prints), product close-ups with raw ingredients (shea, oils, herbs).
- **Imagery to avoid:** Stock photos of white spas, clinical white backgrounds, glossy commercial product shots that don't reflect handmade origin.

## 11. Sierra Leone Market Context (for design and copy decisions)

The Freetown beauty and lifestyle space is competitive but mostly social-media-driven. Direct competitors and points of reference include:

- **Lush Skincare SL** — skincare and cosmetics, Freetown
- **The Beauty Bar SL** — full-service salon and spa
- **Na-Li Spa** — Freetown spa and hair salon
- **Harte Essentials** (Goderich) — perfumes, gifts, beauty essentials with nationwide delivery
- **ShikaGold College of Cosmetology** — beauty training institution

**What this means for SMJ Esthetics' positioning:**

- Most competitors compete on **services** (salons, spas) or **imported product retail**. SMJ Esthetics' edge is being a **maker** — handmade, local, natural. Lean into that hard.
- "Made in Sierra Leone" / "locally crafted" is a meaningful differentiator. Display it prominently.
- The **gift/hamper line** is a strong second pillar — there's healthy demand in Freetown for thoughtful, locally-made corporate and personal gifts, especially around Christmas, Eid, weddings, and Mother's Day.
- **Payment expectations:** Customers will expect to pay via Orange Money, Afrimoney, bank transfer, or cash on delivery. Card processing is rare — don't force an e-commerce checkout if WhatsApp ordering works better.
- **Delivery:** Mention delivery zones (Freetown, nationwide?) clearly. "Nationwide delivery 🇸🇱" is a common and effective phrase in this market.
