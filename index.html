Our Story

Where Wellness
Meets Craftsmanship

SMJ Esthetics began in 2021 with a simple idea: that skincare should be natural, gifts should mean something, and craft should be sustainable.

From our workshop in Freetown, we make small-batch soaps, oils, shea butter, and body scrubs alongside curated hampers, Ankara gift bags, and handmade accessories — all designed to feel good and do good.
